The World Record 49x49x49 Rubik's Cube

This is the world’s highest-order Twisty Puzzle—a 49x49x49 twisty puzzle that I designed, prototyped, and assembled from scratch. The design went through several iterations and hundreds of hours of CAD work to perfect, with a focus on optimizing the internal structure for stability, flexibility, and minimal friction.

The puzzle is comprised of 13,827 individually moving pieces, and took five years to complete. I took on this project without compensation, sponsorship, or external reward. I scripted, filmed, and edited this video, which documents the entire engineering and assembly process.

Tools used: SolidWorks, FDM 3D Printing, Manual Machining, Video Production

    Rat Vaping Nose Port Design

    At Utah State University, I worked in the Odum Lab designing custom research hardware to study voluntary nicotine vapor inhalation in rats. I redesigned the nose port device through 18 iterations, ultimately producing a robust, modular design used in published research.

    In addition to the nose port, I designed and fabricated:

    - Photo Detector Electronic Circuit

    - Solenoid Electronic Component

    - Custom Vape and Solenoid Housing

    - Custom Vacuum Tube Splitters

    - Custom Box for the Photo Detector

    - Various custom 3D-printed spacers and fixtures

    Tools used: SolidWorks, SLA & FDM 3D Printing, Electronics Prototyping, Soldering
