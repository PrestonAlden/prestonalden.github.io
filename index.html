Portfolio Highlights

Welcome to my design portfolio. Here you'll find a selection of engineering projects, prototypes, and creative solutions that reflect my passion for solving complex problems through thoughtful design.

Precision Gearbox Design

This compact gearbox was engineered for low-backlash and high-torque applications in robotics. Designed in Fusion 360 and tested with 3D-printed prototypes to validate tolerances.

World Record Twisty Puzzle

Designed and assembled the highest order Rubik’s Cube in the world (49x49x49). Over 3,000 hours of CAD work, custom support structures, and part optimization went into this Guinness-submitted project.

Telemetry Enclosure

Developed a lightweight, weatherproof casing for a telemetry device used in remote environmental monitoring. Focused on ease of assembly and integration with existing electronics.
